A collection of things I find inspiring

Last night, at the premiere of Werner Herzog's documentary on the internet, someone asked Herzog what he would say as advice to young filmmakers. Herzog replied: "Read, read, read, read, read, read." It reminded me of this 1916 quote from Henry Hazlitt, in his Thinking as a Science: "A man with a scant vocabulary will almost certainly be a weak thinker.
