People who post inspirational quotes are less intelligent

A new scientific study has found that people who enjoy posting "inspirational" statements - eg. that shit that clogs up your Facebook feed every now and then - tend to have lower levels of intelligence. The research, which took place at the University of Waterloo in Canada, examined how receptivity to "pseudo-profound bullshit" related to a person's intellectual level.
